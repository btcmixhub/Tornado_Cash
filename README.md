## How [Tornado Cash](https://tornadocash1.com/) Works

Most **bitcoin mixers** pool funds in a central wallet and send them out to users. Tornado Cash uses a completely different, math-based approach using zero-knowledge proofs (zk-SNARKs). Here is the simple version:

1. **Deposit** — You send your crypto to the Tornado Cash smart contract and receive a unique cryptographic "deposit note" (like a secret password).
2. **Wait** — Your funds sit in the contract's giant liquidity pool, completely disconnected from your identity. Waiting allows other people to deposit and withdraw, growing the anonymity pool.
3. **Withdraw** — You use your secret deposit note from a brand-new, clean wallet. The smart contract uses zero-knowledge math to verify the note is valid without ever revealing which deposit it belongs to. The contract then sends your funds to the new wallet.

Because the deposit and withdrawal are completely detached on the blockchain, there is no visible link between the sender and the receiver.

## Key Features

- **Non-Custodial** — The smart contract holds the funds, but only the person with the deposit note can withdraw them. No operator can steal or freeze your crypto.
- **Immutable Smart Contracts** — Once deployed, the code cannot be updated, deleted, or shut down by anyone.
- **Zero-Knowledge Proofs** — The most advanced privacy tech in crypto, ensuring your transaction history cannot be traced by blockchain analysis.
- **Fixed Pool Sizes** — Tornado Cash uses specific deposit amounts (like 1 ETH, 10 ETH, 100 ETH). Mixing identical amounts makes it impossible to track specific coins based on their value.
- **Relayer Network** — You can use a relayer to pay gas fees from the deposited funds, so your new withdrawal wallet doesn't even need to hold ETH to start, keeping it completely clean.
